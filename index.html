Meet the Chef

Passion on
Every Plate

Born in Lyon and trained under two Michelin-starred kitchens in Paris and Tokyo, Chef Marco Delacroix brings a rare fusion of classical French technique and bold Japanese precision to every dish at Savora.

His philosophy is simple: honour the ingredient, respect the season, and surprise the guest. Each menu changes quarterly to reflect what's freshest, most vibrant, and most exciting.
