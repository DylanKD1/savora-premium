Meet the Chef

Passion on
every plate

Born in Lyon and trained in two Michelin-starred kitchens in Paris and Tokyo, Chef Marco Delacroix brings a rare fusion of classical French technique and bold Japanese precision to every dish at Savora.

His philosophy: honour the ingredient, respect the season, surprise the guest. Each menu changes quarterly to reflect what's freshest and most exciting.
